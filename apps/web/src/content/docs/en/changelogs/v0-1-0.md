Released: 2026-05-10

Yomitomo's first release introduced a local-first AI reading companion experience. The desktop app imported articles, saved reading data, managed LLM providers and reading assistants, and provided reading, annotation, and stats workflows.

## Core Features

- Article reader with import, reading view, table of contents, font size, content width, and annotation sidebar.
- Highlights and annotations with selected-text highlighting, annotation types, comments, and discussion threads.
- AI reading assistants that generate annotations or replies grounded in source text, selections, and discussions.
- Active close reading with one or more assistants reading article sections in an orchestrated flow.
- Library for article body text, source links, annotations, comments, and reading state.
- Reading stats for local trends across articles, annotations, and discussions.
- Local-first storage for reading data, providers, and assistant configuration.

## Desktop

- Built with Electron, including Library, Settings, Assistants, Providers, Stats, and Logs views.
- Saved articles, annotations, comments, providers, and assistants in SQLite.
- Supported OpenAI-compatible, OpenAI Responses, Anthropic, Gemini, and other LLM providers.
- Supported creating and managing reading assistants and review assistants with provider/model routing.
- Provided a centralized desktop workspace for reviewing and managing reading materials.
- Supported review assistants for fact attribution, evidence chains, and coverage checks.
